# Epic 1 — User Story 1.2: View Case Dashboard

**As a** provider
**I want to** see all documents, encounters, and invoices in one place
**So that** I can quickly review the patient case.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Dashboard shows:
  - Uploaded MRI reports
  - Chiropractor reports
  - Generated documents
  - Procedures
  - Billing history
- Documents are searchable
- Timeline view of visits and procedures

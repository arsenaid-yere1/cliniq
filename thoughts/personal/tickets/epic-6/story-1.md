# Epic 6 — Medical Billing & Invoicing

**Goal:** Generate invoices based on visits and procedures performed.

## User Story 6.1 — Create Invoice from Procedure

As a billing staff member
I want to create invoices after discharge, so that we keep track of billing work.

### Acceptance Criteria

- Invoice includes:
  - Patient info
  - Provider info
  - Date of service
  - Line items
